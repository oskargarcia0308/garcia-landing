COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

We've answered the most common questions about our services, process, and pricing.

How long does a typical backyard transformation take? +
Most projects take 1-3 weeks from start to finish depending on scope. Simple turf installations can be completed in 2-3 days, while complete transformations with multiple features typically take 10-14 business days. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the project.
What's included in the free consultation? +
Our free consultation includes an on-site visit, detailed measurements, discussion of your vision and budget, review of material options, and a comprehensive written quote. There's absolutely no obligation—we want you to feel confident in your decision whether you choose us or not.
Do you offer financing options? +
Yes! We work with several financing partners to offer flexible payment plans for qualified customers. Most projects can be financed with approved credit, allowing you to enjoy your new outdoor space now while spreading payments over time. Ask about current promotional rates during your consultation.
How much does artificial turf cost? +
Turf installation typically ranges from $8-15 per square foot depending on turf quality, area size, and site preparation needs. Most residential backyards (500-1000 sq ft) run $5,000-$12,000 fully installed. We use premium turf with 10-year warranties and include proper drainage, weed barrier, and infill for a professional finish that lasts.
Can you work with my HOA requirements? +
Absolutely! We're experienced working with El Paso area HOAs and can help navigate approval processes. We'll provide detailed plans, material specifications, and renderings to submit to your HOA if required. We know what most local HOAs approve and can design accordingly.
What happens if I'm not satisfied with the work? +
Your satisfaction is our top priority. We conduct a detailed walkthrough before considering any project complete. If something isn't exactly right, we'll fix it—no questions asked. We also provide warranties on materials and workmanship. Our reputation is built on happy customers, and we stand behind every project 100%.
Do you handle permits and inspections? +
Yes, we handle all necessary permits and inspections as part of our service. Most landscaping projects don't require permits, but structural work like pergolas and covered patios do. We take care of all the paperwork and ensure everything is done to code so you don't have to worry about it.
How do I maintain artificial turf? +
Turf is incredibly low-maintenance! Simply brush or rake it occasionally to keep fibers upright, rinse with water to remove dust, and spot-clean any pet waste. No mowing, no watering, no fertilizing. We provide complete care instructions and recommend a light cleaning 2-3 times per year for optimal appearance. Your turf will look great for 15-20 years with minimal effort.
